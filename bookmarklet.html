Porque?

Because Latin American leaders need to be held accountable. Because you deserve to know when the people you elected are telling the truth or lying to your face. Because an open and transparent government is better for everyone.

You will be a part of a continent wide movement to let politicians know that they can't use lies as part of their campaign platform. All you need to do is install the bookmarklet - then when you see a website where a politician is making a claim. Just highlight the claim and click the "Mandar a FactoPolitico" bookmarklet.

We will then start tracking this statement for truth & over time, provide evidence to prove or disprove the claim. It's that simple.
